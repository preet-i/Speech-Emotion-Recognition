ABOUT THE PROJECT

The Speech Emotion Recognition (SER) project aims to create a system capable of identifying human emotions based on vocal expressions. This project leverages advanced machine learning algorithms and deep learning techniques to analyze audio signals and recognize emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, and more. By interpreting the emotional tone of speech, the SER system has the potential to enhance human-computer interactions, provide valuable insights in customer service, and support various applications in mental health and well-being.

Applications

  • Customer Service: Improve customer interactions by identifying and responding to customer emotions.
  • Mental Health: Assist therapists and counselors in monitoring patients' emotional states and progress.
  • Education: Enhance e-learning platforms by adapting content based on students' emotional feedback.
  • Entertainment: Personalize user experiences in gaming and virtual reality by adapting to players' emotions.
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Create more empathetic and responsive virtual assistants and chatbots.
